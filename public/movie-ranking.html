The Best Movies According to Morgan

My top 3 movies of all time.

Carol

It's so artfully done. The subtle queer moments throughout that normally would go unmissed by a straight person is just so realistic. Also who doesn't love Cate Blanchette, Rooney Mara, and Sarah Paulson all in one film.

Sweeney Todd

I love the dreary styling of the film. The dark humor is also something I enjoy. And anything Helena Bonhem Carter is in, I'll watch.

Coraline

I love Tim Burton films. The styling is very nostalgic, and I just love stop motion.
